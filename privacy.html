Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 08-07-2025

1. What We Collect

This extension stores URLs and timestamps of visited developer documentation pages such as MDN, npm, React, Node.js, and others.

2. Where Data Is Stored

All collected data is saved locally using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API. No data leaves your browser.

3. Why We Collect It

To help you revisit developer documentation pages you've opened during your browsing session.

4. What We Don't Do

  • We do not send your data to any server
  • We do not use analytics or tracking
  • We do not share your data with third parties
  • We do not load remote scripts or run external code

5. User Control

You can clear your visit history using the "Clear" button in the extension popup. Removing the extension also deletes all stored data.
